Overview

Born in Delhi, India, on November 5, 1988, to Prem Kohli, a criminal lawyer, and Saroj Kohli, a homemaker, Kohli grew up in a middle-class Punjabi family. He began formal cricket training at the West Delhi Cricket Academy at age nine. A pivotal moment in his youth was his father's death in 2006, an event that fueled his determination to pursue cricket with increased seriousness.

Virat Kohli is an iconic Indian cricketer, widely celebrated as one of the greatest batsmen in modern cricket history. Known for his aggressive style and discipline, he has achieved numerous records and led India to historic victories before retiring from T20I and Test formats in 2024 and 2025.
