Records

Virat Kohli holds numerous cricket records, notably being the fastest to numerous ODI milestones (9k, 10k, 11k, 12k runs) and scoring the most ODI centuries (50), surpassing Sachin Tendulkar. He's the only player with all three major ICC Player of the Year awards in one year (2018), has the most Test double centuries (7), and leads in IPL runs and seasons. He's renowned for his consistency, averaging over 50 across formats, and as a successful captain.

First Indian with four centuries in a single overseas Test series (Australia, 2014-15).
