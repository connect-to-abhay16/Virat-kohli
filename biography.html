Biography

Virat Kohli was born November 5, 1988, Delhi. He is an Indian cricketer, widely considered one of the best batsmen of his generation, known for his aggressive style, exceptional fitness, and records across all formats, including leading India to historic wins and becoming the first player to score 50 ODI centuries. Rising from the West Delhi Cricket Academy and captaining India to U-19 World Cup glory in 2008, he debuted for India the same year, transforming fitness standards and leading the national team to significant series victories, particularly in Australia.

He trained at the West Delhi Cricket Academy, excelled at junior levels, and captained India to victory in the 2008 U-19 World Cup.
